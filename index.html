About

Simulate. Experiment. Master Your Finances.

Trinity Capital as a whole provides a unique and effective way to learn about personal finance. Our app is designed to be used by anyone, regardless of age or experience. We believe that everyone should have access to the tools they need to succeed financially, and we are here to provide that.

  • An intuitive solution to the financial literacy crisis

    Trinity Capital tackles financial literacy head on, allowing users to practice the skills they learn in a safe way

  • Effective knwoledge transfer, learn by doing

    Trinity Capital prides itself on its learn by doing approach, allowing users to learn and practice their financial skills through experimentation, trial and error. The lessons users learn through doing are more likely to stick with them than if they were to read a book or watch a video.

With these in mind, Trinity Capital establishes itself as the premier financial literacy tool for anyone looking to learn more about personal finance.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Do bills and payments appear at realistic times?

Yes they do! Trinity Capital is built to provide the most realistic experience as possible. That means when you submit a bill or a paycheck in the simulation, it hits your account when youd expect it to.

Will there be lessons included in the app?

Yes there will be! We are working hard to transform Trinity Capital into a full fledged financial literacy tool. We are working on lessons that will teach users about the basics of personal finance, and how to apply them in the real world.

What the heck is the demo? And why does it look so different from the paid app?

The demo was initially built to show public schools what apps like Trinity Capital can do. But now, I am releasing it to the public so they can try out Trinity Capital for free. As for the look? We had to simplify the demo to make it usable in a classroom setting. We'll get around to updating it here soon.
