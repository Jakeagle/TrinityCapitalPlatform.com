Learn. Practice. Master Financial Skills.

Trinity Capital provides schools with a comprehensive financial literacy education platform. Designed specifically for classroom use, our banking simulation gives students hands-on experience with real-world financial concepts in a safe, controlled environment where mistakes become valuable learning opportunities.

  • Addressing the financial literacy gap in education

    Trinity Capital bridges the gap between theoretical financial concepts and practical application, giving students the confidence to make informed financial decisions

  • Experiential learning that builds lasting knowledge

    Students learn by doing through our realistic banking simulation. This hands-on approach creates deeper understanding and retention compared to traditional textbook-based financial education methods.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Do bills and payments appear at realistic times?

Yes they do! Trinity Capital is built to provide the most realistic experience as possible. That means when you submit a bill or a paycheck in the simulation, it hits your account when youd expect it to.

Will there be lessons included in the app?

Yes there will be! We are working hard to transform Trinity Capital into a full fledged financial literacy tool. We are working on lessons that will teach users about the basics of personal finance, and how to apply them in the real world.

What the heck is the demo? And why does it look so different from the paid app?

The demo was initially built to show public schools what apps like Trinity Capital can do. But now, I am releasing it to the public so they can try out Trinity Capital for free. As for the look? We had to simplify the demo to make it usable in a classroom setting. We'll get around to updating it here soon.
