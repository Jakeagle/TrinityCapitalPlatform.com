About

Find Out More About Us

Simulate. Experiment. Master Your Finances.

Trinity Capital as a whole provides a unique and effective way to learn about personal finance. Our app is designed to be used by anyone, regardless of age or experience. We believe that everyone should have access to the tools they need to succeed financially, and we are here to provide that.

  • An intuitive solution to the financial literacy crisis

    Trinity Capital tackles financial literacy head on, allowing users to practice the skills they learn in a safe way

  • Effective knowledge transfer, learn by doing

    Trinity Capital prides itself on its learn by doing approach, allowing users to learn and practice their financial skills through experimentation, trial and error. The lessons users learn through doing are more likely to stick with them than if they were to read a book or watch a video.

With these in mind, Trinity Capital establishes itself as the premier financial literacy tool for anyone looking to learn more about personal finance.
