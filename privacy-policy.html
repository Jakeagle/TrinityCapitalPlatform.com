Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 19, 2026

1. Introduction

FERGUSON SOFTWARE SOLUTIONS (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy and security of the students and schools that use our educational software, Trinity Capital (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, and use Student Data, and the measures we take to safeguard it.

2. Definitions

For the purposes of this Privacy Policy:

  • Student Data: Information directly related to a student maintained by FERGUSON SOFTWARE SOLUTIONS on behalf of a School, including names, grades, lesson progress, and simulated financial data such as accounts, transactions, paychecks, bills, and budgets.
  • School: Any public school, district, or educational institution authorized to use the Service.
  • Teacher/Administrator: School personnel authorized to manage classes and students within the Service.

3. Data Collection

We collect only the information necessary to provide educational services:

  • Student names, grades, lesson progress, and completion data.
  • Simulated financial data created within the Service (e.g., fake checking accounts, paychecks, bills, budgets) strictly for educational purposes.
  • Teacher and school identifiers for class management.

We do not collect real financial information, social security numbers, or health data.

4. Use of Student Data

Student Data is used solely to:

  • Deliver lessons and interactive simulations.
  • Track student progress and grades.
  • Provide teachers with tools to monitor classroom engagement.
  • Generate reports for educational purposes only.

5. Data Storage and Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect Student Data, including:

  • Encrypted storage of Student Data at rest using MongoDB Atlas.
  • Encrypted transmission of data using TLS 1.2+.
  • Role-based access control to ensure only authorized teachers, administrators, and personnel can access relevant Student Data.
  • Logging and auditing access to Student Data for compliance purposes.
  • Secure management of credentials, API keys, and environment variables via Railway.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise share Student Data outside of the intended educational use. Student Data may be shared with:

  • Teachers and administrators authorized by the School to manage classes.
  • Third-party service providers only as necessary to operate the Service, under contractual agreements ensuring FERPA compliance.

7. Data Retention

Student Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide educational services. Upon request or at the end of the School’s use of the Service, Student Data will be returned to the School or securely deleted.

8. Student Rights

Schools and students have the following rights:

  • Access to their Student Data through authorized accounts.
  • Request corrections or deletions of inaccurate or outdated data.
  • Request information about how their data is stored and protected.

9. Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a confirmed data breach, FERGUSON SOFTWARE SOLUTIONS will promptly notify the affected School in accordance with FERPA, Texas law, and applicable regulations, typically within 48 hours of discovery.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Schools will be notified of significant changes. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
